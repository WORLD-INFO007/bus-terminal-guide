Why this page can't show live departure times

Port Authority is a shared terminal — it doesn't run its own buses. Every route is operated independently by NJ Transit, Greyhound, Megabus, and other private carriers, and each maintains its own live schedule that changes by day, holiday, and season. Rather than list times that could be wrong by the time you read them, this page links directly to each carrier's official real-time schedule tool below.

How to find your exact departure time

  1. Identify which carrier runs your route (see list above, or the full carrier directory).
  2. Open that carrier's website and enter your origin and destination.
  3. Note your gate number — Port Authority gates change periodically, so confirm on the day of travel via the terminal's digital boards or the carrier's app.
  4. Arrive at least 20–30 minutes early during peak commuting hours (7–9 AM and 4–7 PM), when gate lines are longest.

Getting to the terminal

The terminal sits one block west of Times Square, between 8th and 9th Avenues and 40th–42nd Streets. It connects directly to the A, C, and E subway lines, with fully accessible entry via the north wing between 41st and 42nd Streets. Free Wi-Fi ("_Free PABusTerminal WiFi") is available for the first 30 minutes.
